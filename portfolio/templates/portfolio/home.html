About me


Growing up, I dreamt of becoming an author, writing my own books for others to read. I used to write (and draw) my own books before bringing them to class for others to enjoy. When did writing stories turn into writing code? Honestly, I don't know. But one way or another, I was destined to write.

I'm currently a second-year computer science student at Ryerson University. My interests lie in web development, data analytics and software engineering. And while a lot of my work is coding-based, I enjoy trying out many different things, from business to design to athletics. When I'm not completing assignments or learning new technology, I'm attending hackathons and case competitions to put my skills to the test.

On this site, you'll find a snapshot of the work I do along with a little bit about myself. You may also find my resume above (document icon). For any opportunities, please contact me here or through one of my socials listed.
